About

The AMC are an AXR project dedicated to making and managing Axian media, like the Axia's equivelent of the BBC. Primarily the AMC focuses on books, such as Admiral Rocket's biography, and other educational resources relating to the Rubicona War and AXU. The AMC is closely related to AxosNews and the Axian Building Society.
